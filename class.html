ACE's approach to education is completely different from other curricula because it is Bible based and proves that indeed the "fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom". Its key pillars are; Goal setting, Individualized and Mastery-based, Biblical Values,Diagnostic Testing, Character Building,Academic Excellence, Critical Thinking Skills and Academic Prescription approach to learning. It provides a holistic education that touches spirit, souls and body.

ACE avoids the exam-based and teacher centered models of education which does't take care of the individual learning needs and gifting of each student, character development, personal responsibility and mastery of knowledge as opposed to cramming. ACE provides a pathway to university education globally. As of 2022, "Graduates from the ACE program are attending more than 1,400 colleges and universities globally with outstanding performance".

About Admissions

ESIS Admission

Thank you for your interest in Es Shemen International School (ESIS). If you have not already done so, and are in Uganda, we invite you to come and visit our beautiful campus at Plot 9B Dokolo Road at the intersection with Boroboro Road, Senior Quarters B, Lira City east Division.

Admission process

ESIS has a “rolling admission” process which means students may enrol at any time during the school year. While we understand that securing school enrolment is a priority for families, we cannot do so until we have received complete applications and reviewed each child’s file for admission. Please visit our school or call +256-777230537 to book an appointment.

Application Fees

Our application fees

To complete an application to ESIS, the application fee of UGX 150,000 per child (Registration, 50,000/=, Application, 50,000/= Diagnostic Test 50,000/=) needs to be deposited to the school's account. Payment may be made by bank transfer or direct deposit to any of the school bank accounts provided below, or though the school business office by cheque.Please ensure that the student’s family name is indicated when making a payment and if possible send a copy of the transfer or deposit slip to bursar@esis.ac.ug

Our placement of grades

Students are generally placed in grades based on the age of the child. Please note that we also consider previous school transcripts (where applicable), academic ability, social development, interviews and placement tests (where necessary) to determine grade placement at ESIS.

You may download the application forms by clicking the links found below, Fill it and return it to the admissions office OR send the e-copy to admissions@esis.ac.ug with supporting documents including evidence of payment.
